PHARMACY POLICY (DTI & GENERAL COMPLIANCE)

Business Compliance

  • This pharmacy operates in accordance with the Department of Trade and Industry (DTI) regulations and maintains a valid Business Permit and DTI Registration.
  • All transactions follow the Consumer Act of the Philippines (RA 7394) and related pharmaceutical laws.

Product Authenticity and Safety

  • All medicines sold are FDA-approved and sourced from licensed distributors.
  • We do not sell expired, tampered, or unregistered drugs.
  • Customers are encouraged to check packaging and expiration dates upon receipt.

Pricing and Discounts

  • Prices are set according to DTI Fair Pricing Guidelines.
  • Senior Citizens and PWD discounts are honored under RA 9994 and RA 10754.
  • Discounted transactions require valid ID presentation.

Refunds and Returns

  • Refunds are allowed only for damaged, incorrect, or defective items upon delivery.
  • Medicines cannot be returned or exchanged once opened, unless proven defective or dispensed in error.
  • Customers must present a receipt for refund or replacement within 7 days.

Data Privacy

  • Customer data are collected and processed in compliance with the Data Privacy Act of 2012.
  • Personal information is used only for transaction and order fulfillment purposes.

Customer Service

  • Complaints and refund requests can be filed through the pharmacy's contact form or email.
  • Operating hours: Monday - Saturday, 8:00 AM to 8:00 PM.

Policy Updates

  • The pharmacy reserves the right to update these policies in compliance with DTI or FDA regulations.
