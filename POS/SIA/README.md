Functional Requirements
Functional Requirements for Pharmacy & Hospital POS System
1. User Management & Security
Role-based access control (e.g., admin, pharmacist, cashier, doctor, nurse, inventory staff).
Secure login & authentication (multi-factor authentication for sensitive roles).
Audit logs to track user activities and system changes.
Data privacy compliance (HIPAA, GDPR, or local equivalents).

2. Sales & Transaction Management
Process cash, card, digital wallet, and insurance-based transactions.
Prescription-based sales (pharmacy validates prescriptions before dispensing).
Over-the-counter (OTC) sales without prescription.
Printing (with hospital/pharmacy branding).
Discounts, promotions, and loyalty programs.
Refunds and returns management with prescription validation.
Integration with insurance providers (claim verification, billing codes).

3. POS Inventory  Management
Real-time stock updates during sales.
Batch & expiry date tracking (alerts for near-expiry or expired medicines).
Stock-in/stock-out management for suppliers and hospital wards.
Automatic re-order alerts when stock falls below threshold.
Drug categorization & search filters (generic, brand, dosage form, therapeutic class).
Supplier & purchase order management.

6. Reporting & Analytics
Daily, weekly, and monthly sales reports.
Profit & revenue analysis (by product, category, supplier).
Patient/doctor prescribing trends.
Regulatory compliance reports 

7. System Features & Usability
Web-based & mobile-friendly interface (Uses HTML, CSS, JQUERY, PHP, JAVASCRIPT)
Barcode & QR code scanning for fast checkout.
Multi-language support for diverse users.
Offline mode
Cloud deployment option for scalability.
Customizable dashboards for different user roles.


8. Advanced / Optional Features

Integration with digital marketing 
Regulatory submission automation
